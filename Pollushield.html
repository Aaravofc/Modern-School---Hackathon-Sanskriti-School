About Our Products

Air pollution has become a major problem in the world today. The human civilisation is coming closer to extinction everyday due to growing pollution in the atmosphere. These harmful pollutants like Sulphur Dioxide and Ammonia, which can cause respiratory problems and liver diseases. We must find a solution to this problem soon.

Our company has found a solution. We make fabric which is induced with pure activated carbons which absorb pollutants from the atmosphere without harming our body. It effectively removes pollutants, elimantes odour, acts as a barrier against harmful chemicals and also presnts alergens present in the air. Also, it is made using eco-friendly fibres or through the use of recycled or plat-based fibres which are eco-friendly.

Our company provides people with the oppurtunity to fight pollution and at the same time be fashionable. So, if you want to support our cause, then we strongly urge you to buy our clothes and fabrics.
