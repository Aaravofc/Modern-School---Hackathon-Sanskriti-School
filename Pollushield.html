About Our Products

Introducing the revolutionary fabric made of activated carbon, designed to absorb carbon dioxide and protect the environment. Our innovative fabric is not only eco-friendly but also features a hydrophobic coating, ensuring it remains resistant to stains and makes it easy to wipe them off before any mishaps. Mostly crafted from biodegradable threads, it keeps the environment safe when ready to dispose. Embrace this fabric and make a positive impact on the environment while enjoying its fashionable sense!
